# AI Patient-Owned Care Continuity Platform (Health OS)

## Vision
Build a secure, patient-centric healthcare platform where individuals own, manage, and securely share their complete medical history. The platform improves continuity of care by providing a single source of truth for health records and enhances understanding through AI-powered report summarization and health insights.

## Problem Statement
Patients often receive care from multiple hospitals, clinics, and laboratories. Medical records become fragmented, making it difficult for doctors to access complete patient histories. This can lead to duplicate tests, treatment delays, and less informed clinical decisions.

## Target Users
- Patients
- Doctors
- Healthcare Administrators (future)
- Laboratories (future)

## Success Criteria
- Patients can securely manage their records.
- Doctors can access shared records with patient permission.
- Medical reports can be uploaded and organized.
- AI generates understandable summaries.
- The application is secure, scalable, and production-ready.
